The multi-pair unlock

Readers customers don't need one pair. They need one for every room, every bag, every occasion. The intent is already there — it just needs the right trigger at the right moment.

Replenishment isn't optional

Lenses scratch. Trends shift. Styles change. eyebobs customers will come back — but only if you're present when that window opens. Most brands aren't.

Gifting is a lifecycle, not a campaign

"Teacher Appreciation," Mother's Day, birthdays — stylish readers are one of the easiest gifted accessories in the market. Brands that build gifting flows (not just blasts) own that moment every year.

Loyalty programs are only as powerful as the engine behind them

Frame Fanatics is an asset. Points systems without behavior-triggered communication just collect dust. The brands winning with loyalty connect every earn, every tier, every redemption to a personalized email or SMS.

Post-purchase is where LTV is decided

The customer who understands why their frames are worth it — the Italian acetate, the spring hinges, the craftsmanship — buys again. The one who doesn't, doesn't. Education is revenue.
