Five Findings That Matter
The fastest paths to more revenue.
Each one is confirmed, scoped, and fixable.
01
The winback flow has never sent.
$0 revenue. 0 deliveries. The trigger is set to a 100-day order delay — meaning no existing customer has ever triggered it. Every lapsed customer is sitting unreached. This is the single fastest revenue recovery in the program.
02
60% of your best customers aren't in the loyalty program.
Your loyalists average 12+ purchases. 60% have never enrolled in Frame Fanatics. They have points, they have tier status — and they have no idea. The program launched January 2025. Pre-existing customers were never enrolled, and the infrastructure for re-enrollment was never built.
03
Campaign revenue is down 20.9% YoY — not from under-sending, from under-targeting.
eyebobs sends email 5+ days per week. Revenue per recipient is flat YoY ($0.047 both years). The decline comes from fewer total recipients reached (16M vs 20.4M). VIP/segmented sends return $0.25–$0.58 RPR vs. $0.04–$0.08 for broad sends — a 5–10x difference. The fix is smarter segmentation, not more volume.
04
New subscribers are getting 12.8 emails in their first 7 days.
Welcome flow + campaign sends fire simultaneously, creating a chaos window. A real subscriber received "Sale Access = Granted," abandon cart, and four "25% off ends tonight" emails in the first 5 days. She converted — but it took 36 days and an enormous amount of email volume. There's a better way.
05
The Points Reminder made $46 in 6 months.
It should be a top-3 earner. Smart sending is suppressing 99% of sends. Right now, 10,811 profiles have redeemable points they don't know about — the equivalent of a store full of customers holding gift cards they forgot they had.
