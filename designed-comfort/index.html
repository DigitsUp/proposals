Google My Business — Own the Map

When someone Googles "HVAC Hampton Bays" or "AC repair Hamptons," three businesses show up on the map at the top of the results. Everything below that gets about 10% of the clicks. Google My Business is how you get in those three spots — and right now, Designed Comfort isn't there.

We set up, verify, and fully optimize your Google Business Profile. Category, service list, photos, service area by town, weekly posts, and a review-generation strategy that gets you to the top of the local map pack before winter.

93% of local service searches show the Google Map Pack first. Everything below it fights for the remaining 7%.
Local SEO — Own the Search

A single-page website gets indexed once. A website with 10 service pages gets 10 separate chances to rank. We build dedicated landing pages for every service and every major town in your service area.

"AC Repair Hampton Bays." "Furnace Installation East Hampton." "Ductless Mini-Split Sag Harbor." Each page is a new door into your business — and each one is a keyword your competitors aren't owning yet.

  • Service pages for every offering
  • Location pages for every major town served
  • Blog content targeting seasonal search intent — "best time to service your AC on Long Island"
  • AI search optimization — built to show up in ChatGPT and Google AI answers, not just traditional search
