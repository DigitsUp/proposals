What's working

  • ✓
    Email is carrying the business. 58% of all revenue is email-attributed, and it grew +40% — faster than the store itself.
  • ✓
    Flows compounded +50%. Added to Cart, Abandoned Checkout and the Marry Me Chicken welcome are all firing well above last period.
  • ✓
    Engagement is healthier. Campaign open rate climbed to 56.8% (+10 pts) while unsubscribes fell — a more receptive list, not a fatigued one.
  • ✓
    Loyalty is building. Returning-customer rate hit 45.84% (+9%), led by an affiliate channel that punches far above its size.

Where revenue is leaking

  • !
    Conversion fell as traffic rose. Sessions +25% but CVR −0.8% — the contest & new-arrival traffic is high-volume, lower-intent. The checkout isn't keeping pace.
  • !
    The retention flows are switched off. Every Winback, the full Post-Purchase series, Back-in-Stock and Review-Request sit in draft — earning CA$0.
  • !
    Second orders are the gap. 60% of DTC and 50% of affiliate-referred buyers never return — exactly the people winback flows exist to recover.
  • !
    SMS is at zero. 100% of attributed revenue is email; a mobile-first audience (69%) has an untouched channel.

04 — The Moves That Matter

Where performance meets persona.

Three priorities that connect what the data shows to what Amelia can write this week.

Move 01

Switch on the winback & post-purchase flows.

The retention flows are already built and sitting in draft — earning nothing. They target the precise leak: 60% of DTC and 50% of referred buyers never return. Turning them on, with a free anchor product (CCB, Banana Bread), is the fastest revenue in this audit.

For · DTC & Customers of Affiliates
Move 02

Make the second-order email a flagship.

The Marry Me Chicken welcome proves the model: one famous product, outcome in the name, low risk. Apply that voice to a dedicated second-purchase email — built around the obvious next product beside what they first bought.

For · Customers of Affiliates
Move 03

Open a path from buyer to affiliate.

Affiliates are 8% of profiles but touch 45% of orders. The highest-value referred customers already buy on a 22-day cadence without being asked. Identify the affiliate-behaving and invite them in — the channel is the brand's biggest compounding asset.

For · Affiliates & near-affiliates
